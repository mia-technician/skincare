❄️ CoolSculpting / TruSculpt Technology

KEY FACTS CoolSculpting is a fat reduction treatment for areas resistant to diet and exercise.

  • Not a weight loss treatment (like gastric bypass) — not a surgical procedure (like liposuction).
  • Non-surgical localized fat reduction, FDA & Health Canada Approved.
  • 8 million+ treatments performed worldwide.
  • Clinical study shows 20% reduction of fat cells in a single treatment.
  • Most patients reach goal in 3 treatments; a few may need more.
  • Fat cells crystallize (freeze) → break down → die → eliminated by lymphatic system within 6 months.
  • Results visible from 3 weeks; reassess at 3 months.
  • Fat cells are gone permanently. However, remaining fat cells can enlarge with weight gain — maintaining stable weight is key.

⚠️ Side Effects

Common side effects (few hours to 2 weeks): redness, bruising, deep itching, tingling, numbness, tenderness, pain, strong cramping, nausea, bloating, diarrhea, muscle spasms, aching. Numbness may persist up to 2 months.

Rare side effects informed:

  • Darker skin colour (can occur on darker skin type, resolves on its own, or accelerated with Lytera).
  • Hardness or discrete nodules — massage may help.
  • Paradoxical Adipose Hyperplasia (PAH) — enlargement of treated area, extremely rare (0.2%), requires surgical intervention.
  • Hernia — requires surgical intervention.

📋 Treatment Process

  • Shave or trim long hair on treatment areas at least 1 day prior.
  • Wear loose clothing, slip-on shoes.
  • Eat a full breakfast, bring snacks (empty stomach may cause headache/nausea). Lunch provided if full-day treatment.
  • Sign consents, take photos, mark area with semi-permanent markers (washes off in days).
  • Measure and record patient's weight.
  • One applicator per session (35–75 min). Multiple applicators may take half or full day — bathroom breaks, laptop/phone use, or napping are fine.
  • Pre-wipe area with anti‑freeze solution, apply gel/gel pad to protect skin.
  • Applicator suctions area into cup: first few minutes feel cold/prickling/tingling → then area goes numb.
  • After treatment: 2‑minute massage to break down crystallized fat cells (uncomfortable but manageable).
  • Return to work / normal activities immediately after treatment.
